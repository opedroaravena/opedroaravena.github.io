Fitt's Law

Cubos Academy

cubos.academy

Cubos Academy's goal is to get the user to create an account and become a student. Therefore, following Fitt's law, Cubos Academy put a relatively large "Become a st"udent button right in the middle of the page.

Contrast

Church of Jesus Christ

churchofjesuschrist.org

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints wants young adult users to remember about and participate in this devotional by Elder and Sister Gong. The image behind the announcement is darkened, and the text is white. This provides nice contrast and draws the user's attention.

Hick's Law

Netshoes

netshoes.com.br/

Netshoes wants its users to see all the best products on the homepage so they won't get lost among so many options. They either want the user to learn more or buy something on the website, so those are the buttons available.
