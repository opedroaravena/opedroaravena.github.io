Persona 1

A young woman who lives close to her work and rides her bycicle everyday to office. She has to check the weather weekly so she can organize herself and decide which days the week she will go by car or riding her bycicle.

Persona 2

An entrepreneur that owns a company that installs cable TV's all around the city, he needs to work while it is not raining, otherwise it gets really risky to climb houses and roofs, checking the best option will be useful, he can use the website.

Scenarios

  1. The bad weather will affect my team's ability to work today. How bad is it really?
  2. I am hoping to ride my bycicle to work, will it be possible?.
  3. If there is a snow day today, I will have to go by car.
  4. Should I bring an umbrella to the job site today?
